About me

I am currently pursuing the Ph.D degree in Computer Science and Engineering at Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU), advised by Prof. Lizhuang Ma in Digital Media & Computer Vision Laboratory (DMCV). I also receive supervision from Dr. Xin Tan, who is based at East China Normal University (ECNU). I received my bachelor degree in Computer Science and Engineering at Chongqing University (CQU) in 2022.

My research interests include foundation models, open-world learning, and generalized class discovery.
